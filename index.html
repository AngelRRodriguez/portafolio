Welcome, I’m Ángel Rodríguez, Designer & Photographer

I was born in Dominican Republic and have spent my entire life sucking up art and styles from other countries, I love futurism and, only sometimes, minimalism, for I reflect the most colorful and surrealist in my works. I have taken many courses of photography and design, along with currently learning publicity and open to every kind of creative work, wether to sell or to inspire. I am most fulfilled in photography but have experience with every modern program or medium of design and creation and have enough soul to fix almost every problem any client might need to solve.
